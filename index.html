Where to Work ?

A recent survey conducted by Employment Hero revealed that a staggering 72% of Malaysians express a desire to work abroad. Their motivation stems from factors such as a weak exchange rate, inadequate compensation, and extended working hours in Malaysia. In this visualization, our aim is to delve into the working conditions and opportunities available in various countries, shedding light on the factors that drive individuals to seek employment overseas.

World GDP 2023

A country's GDP reflects the overall economic health and prosperity of the nation. Generally, countries with higher GDP per capita tend to have better job opportunities, higher salaries, and a higher standard of living.

Correlation between Wage,Work Life Balance and Life Satisfaction

In general, higher salaries tend to be associated with better work-life balance and higher life satisfaction. Northern Europe and Western Europe are the best regions to go if you are looking for a high Count and a work-life balance working environment.
